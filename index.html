Live Your Best Life - TODAY!

There comes a time in all of our lives, whether admitted or ignored,LIFE HAPPENS! The question is - What do you do when IT happens?

Do you take it like a champ and keep on keeping on? Do you pause or stop life in its tracks? We all handle situations differently. But the key is to keep living. Learn from it and do whatever you must to avoid the black-hole syndrome. Avoid the downward spiral to no where.

To live your best life requires strength, courage, and self-respect. Life is precious, so appreciate the simple things. Celebrate the wins and learn from the losses.

Living your best life starts with:

  • Learning: Grow and develop yourself. Learn from the misses and try try again.
  • Exploring: Try something new. Live outside your comfort zone from time to time.
  • Forgiving: Forgiveness results in lower levels of anxiety, depression and physical problems.
  • Staying True: Stop trying to please others and be true to yourself. Take responsibility when you screw up and be honest even when it upsets someone you love
  • Cleansing: Remove negativity from your life and toss out self doubt and
  • BELIEVING: Believe in yourself.

Confidence is the foundation to acheiving what you want in your life. My best life is different from your best life. I may enjoy binge watching Netflix on Saturday. Whereas you may enjoy being a soccer mom.

When IT happens to you come up swinging. Life is too short, so live in the present moment. Discover your life purpose and create your own opportunities. Enjoy life!


Laugh often...

The proverb, ‘laughter is the best medicine’, means that ‘laughter is the best way to recover’. The implication of this proverb is that ‘laughter is better at curing us than medication.’.

Laughter provides health and healing benefits to your body. It connects the body, mind, and spirit. Laughing releases endorphins, the body's natural feel-good chemicals, reducing pain, releasing tension, and improving your emotional state.

Some benefits of laughter include:

  • Boost immunity
  • Lowers stress hormones
  • Prevents heart disease
  • Strengthens relationships
  • Oxygenates the blood

Unlike when we were children, as we age we’re more serious and lose the beneficial habit of laughing frequently. Laughter gives an immediate release of tension and uplifts the spirits.

GEAR UP: Surround yourself with friends and family and choose to laugh often in your daily life.


Learn everything...

In today’s competitive world mastering new skills are not optional. Joseph Weintraub, coauthor of the book, The Coaching Manager: Developing Top Talent in Business, agrees: “We need to constantly look for opportunities to stretch ourselves in ways that may not always feel comfortable at first. Continual improvement is necessary to get ahead.”

Gone are the days of graduating from high school and never picking up another book. When my mom was a young adult, a high school education was all you would need to get that job and live comfortably. TODAY! You have to be a lifetime student in order to stay ahead of the competition.

Learning is a way of nurturing your brain. Some other benefits of learning something new are:

  • Learning builds relationships
  • Learning speed increases
  • Fights off boredom
  • Keeps you current in your industry

    • Learning new things is a lot of your brain. Stop and think about what you want enhance or learn from scratch.

  • Learn for where your are
  • Learn for where you want to be
  • Most importantly - Learn for YOURSELF!
